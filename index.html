WHAT WE OFFER OUR PATIENTS

Orpthalmology

We provide quality eye care of patients through various clinical services and provide ophthalmic continuing medical education to doctors.

Pharmacy

We cover all pharmaceutical services relating to patient care including assessment of prescription and costing and patient counseling on medicine.

Physiotheraphy
